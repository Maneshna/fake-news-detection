About Model

How the Model Works

The fake news detection model analyzes the textual content of a news article and predicts whether it is real or fake.

Algorithm

A machine learning classifier is trained on a dataset of real and fake news articles.

Input

User enters the content of a news article into the text box for analysis.

Output

The system predicts whether the article is real or fake along with confidence details.
